Conclusions I

  • In larval zebrafish UV cones are specialized by region
  • UV cones in "strike zone" → prey capture:
    • ↑ density
    • ↑ outer segments
    • ↑ detectability of UV-bright stimuli
    • ↳ changes in phototransduction + [Ca2+]
  • UV cones in "horizon zone" (Dorsal/Nasal) → predator detection
  • "strike zone" ≈ primate fovea?

Conclusions II

  • In larval zebrafish UV cones are specialized by region
  • UV cones in "strike zone" → prey capture
  • Bipolar cells in "strike zone" display UV-on responses (Zimmerman, 2018)
  • Ganglion cells in "strike zone" display UV-on responses (Zhou, 2020)
  • RGCs are more dense in SZ
  • AF7 tectal area (prey capture) is innervated mainly by temporal RGCs
  • "strike zone" ≈ primate fovea?
